About the Team

Our Story, Mission, and the People Behind Onyx

Our Story

Founded in Newark, New Jersey, Onyx Dance Crew was created with a mission to build a community of passionate creatives and provide a platform for dancers who bring ingenuity, precision, and emotion to every performance.

From the beginning, the crew has focused on cultivating an environment where artistry and expression can genuinely thrive. Onyx is known for its energetic choreography, diverse styles, and dedication to inspiring others through dance. Many members are students or emerging professionals, and the crew offers them a space to channel their creativity and maintain balance in their busy lives.

Under the direction of Kayla Francis and Julian Santiago, the group has developed into a dynamic movement that celebrates individuality while emphasizing teamwork and discipline. Their leadership has helped shape a culture where dancers can push boundaries and support one another’s growth. What began as a small group of dedicated performers has evolved into a recognized dance collective that now appears across the tri-state area. Today, Onyx brings together artists from diverse backgrounds, united by a shared passion for movement, expression, and community.

Our Mission

"At Onyx, our mission is to unite dancers through innovation, self-expression, and a shared purpose."

Innovation

We strive to create an inclusive space where artists of all backgrounds can train, perform, and push creative limits together.

Self-Expression

Every routine we perform tells a story — one of strength, collaboration, and passion that resonates with audiences and performers alike.

Shared Purpose

We believe in the power of dance to build community, foster creativity, and inspire positive change in the world around us.

What We Do

Performances

Onyx performs at showcases, college events, and community festivals across New Jersey and the surrounding area. Our routines blend hip-hop, contemporary, and street styles, reflecting the versatility and talent of our members.

Workshops & Training

Beyond the stage, we host auditions, workshops, and outreach programs to connect with new dancers and support the local arts community. Our training programs focus on technique, creativity, and performance skills.

Meet the Directors

Kayla Francis

Co-Director & Choreographer

Kayla leads Onyx with vision and dedication, cultivating a culture of creativity and respect that encourages every member to find their voice through movement. Her choreography combines artistry and athleticism, making every Onyx performance both visually powerful and emotionally engaging.

Julian Santiago

Co-Director

Julian brings artistic innovation and technical expertise to Onyx, specializing in contemporary and hip-hop fusion styles. His approach to choreography emphasizes storytelling through movement, creating pieces that connect with audiences on multiple levels.
