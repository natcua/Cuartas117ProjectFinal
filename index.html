Welcome to Onyx Dance Crew

Welcome to the Onyx Dance Crew website, the home to our revolution through movement. Founded in New Jersey, we are an elite community of passionate and driven dancers dedicated to bringing creativity, precision, and raw emotion to every performance. Under the direction of Kayla Francis and Julian Santiago, Onyx has grown into a dynamic movement that celebrates individuality, empowers every dancer, and emphasizes unmatched teamwork and discipline. We move to not just perform, but to inspire.
