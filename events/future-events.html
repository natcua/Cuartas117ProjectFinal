Want to join us for a performance?

For our dancers: some expectations for performances include actively learning and knowing the choreography (we are always available to help), arriving on time, being fully prepared with the proper costumes, and maintaining a positive attitude. Most importantly, stay connected with the team, support one another, and have fun!

Even if you're not dancing with us on stage we would love to have you come support us too, we hope to see you soon!
